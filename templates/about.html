About

The market for craft beer in the U.S. continues to grow, and consumers' willingness to spend more for variety, flavor and quality has only increased since modern craft brewing began in the 1960s. Craft beers typically feature higher quality ingredients, providing drinkers with a more unique and flavorous experience than mass-produced options. Those reaching for a craft beer are adventurous, and often looking to try something new, certainly with no shortage of choices. The number of independent breweries operating in the U.S. reached an all-time high in 2021, with 9,118 different locations(!!) producing their own brew(s) (Brewers Association). For perspective, there were about 13,000 McDonald's locations nationwide that same year (Statista), and there are currently just 3,062 Chipotle locations in the United States (ScrapeHero). So yeah, people like their beer.


But with so many beers and breweries to choose from, finding the right one can feel impossible. That's where we come in. Our goal is to answer your first question when you get to the bar or the beer aisle - "What should I get?" To do so, we've developed this Beer Recommendation app, using a Beer Profile and Ratings Data Set, to find the perfect beer for you based on your taste preferences. With taste profiles and consumer reviews for over 3000 unique beers and 934 breweries, we're confident we can find you the right brew.


Cheers.
